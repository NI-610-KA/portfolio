Here's a time line of Emma Raducanu's life

  • Emma Raducanu was born on 13 November 2002 in Toronto, Canada
  • Raducanu started playing tennis at the age of five
  • Raducanu won the ITF Chandigarh Lawn Tennis girls tournament at the beginning of 2018
  • Raducanu turned professional in 2018
  • She alternated between junior and professional tournaments during 2018 and 2019
  • In 2019 Raducanu competed in the Solapur Open in Maharashtra
  • In the final at Deccan Gymkhana club, she won against Niktah Bains in three sets.
  • She won the LTA British Tour Masters title in December 2020
